# 1 year anniversary plan

## overview

This project will be a timeline webpage that will take the user through the past year of my girlfriend and my realtionship through pictures and notes. The top of the page will have a nav bar that is a timeline showing what date you're on that also works as a 'jump to date' when clicked. There will be a hero with a title [sweet message placeholder: "Our past year together"] below that will have a constant rotating caraousell of pictures of us together. The timeline will be some kind of snap scroll or scroll will control the animation so that each date fades in an out according to scroll (will experiment to see which one feels better). The timeline will scroll from top to bottom with pictures fading and animating into place and the user scrolls to into that date with smooths transitions in and out of each date. For larger events where many memories were made in a small amount of time (camping, vacation) the scroll will shift into a left to right scroll showing all the pictures from that trip. Instrumental music will be playing as well, just a few songs of choice to set the mood.

## objective 

- User (most likely my girlfriend) can scroll through the webpage and be presented with a smooth timeline of our past year
- User can click on the nav dates to be taken to the closest date with an image

## layout 

- vibe: light mode only, cloudy vibe, a year of memories, feels like a dream in clouds
- color scheme: off-white, pink, red, baby blue
- details: soft corners, borders for standouts, maybe some fun user interactions throughout
